# Final Insights Report

## Key Findings

* Marketing campaigns generated significant revenue across multiple channels.
* Search and Influencer channels demonstrated strong performance.
* ROI analysis helped identify the most profitable campaigns.
* Conversion funnel analysis highlighted customer drop-off patterns.
* KPI tracking enabled evaluation of campaign efficiency and profitability.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase investment in high-ROI channels.
* Optimize underperforming campaigns.
* Improve conversion rates through targeted marketing strategies.
* Continuously monitor campaign KPIs for better decision-making.

## Conclusion

The project successfully analyzed marketing campaign performance using data analytics techniques and interactive Power BI dashboards, enabling data-driven marketing decisions.
